About AIMM

A visual AI pipeline for scalable child nutrition monitoring.

AIMM uses smartphone imaging, pose-guided feature extraction, and AI-assisted inference to support visual anthropometry in field settings.

AI-assisted visual anthropometry for child nutrition monitoring using smartphone imaging.

Computational · scientific · humanitarian · field-oriented
01

Data collection

Front and side smartphone images are captured in field conditions.

02

Pose & body detection

Landmarks, contours, and segmentation masks locate body structure.

03

Feature extraction

Anthropometric ratios and structured visual features are computed.

04

AI model

Inference models translate visual features into nutritional signals.

05

Outputs

Risk indicators support monitoring of wasting, stunting, and underweight.
