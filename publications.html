D2A: a community-led smartphone tool for malnutrition screening in Kenya

Bhavnani, R., & Link, N. S. Frontiers in Public Health.

Timely assessments of child acute malnutrition are essential for effective treatment and prevention. The paper introduces D2A, a smartphone workflow to enable regular self-collection of Family MUAC and key correlates of wasting.

Trajectories of resilience to acute malnutrition in the Kenyan drylands

Bhavnani, R., Schlager, N., Reul, M., & Donnay, K. Frontiers in Sustainable Food Systems.

The study develops an outcome-based measure of resilience to acute malnutrition using longitudinal data from Kenya between 2016 and 2020.

Household behavior and vulnerability to acute malnutrition in Kenya

Bhavnani, R., Schlager, N., Donnay, K., Reul, M., Schenker, L., Stauffer, M., & Patel, T. Humanities & Social Sciences Communications.
