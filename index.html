Artificial Intelligence for Monitoring Malnutrition (AIMM)

AIMM develops a smartphone-based system for early detection and monitoring of child acute malnutrition using computer vision and anthropometric data.

The project combines artificial intelligence, infrared imaging, and community-led data collection to enable faster and more accessible nutritional monitoring in low-resource settings.

Funded by a Swiss National Science Foundation BRIDGE Discovery grant.
