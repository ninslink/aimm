Interview with Nina Link

The Artificial Intelligence for Monitoring Malnutrition (AIMM) project aims to make the early detection of child undernutrition faster, more accurate, and more accessible—especially in low-resource or crisis settings where conventional screening tools are difficult to use at scale.

AIMM bridges AI and infrared imaging technology with local collaboration in Maharashtra, India. The project combines technological innovation with co-design and training so caregivers can use the app confidently and safely.
