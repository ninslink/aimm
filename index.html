07.08.2026

First AIMM Technical Advisory Group meeting announced at FLAME University

The first AIMM Technical Advisory Group meeting will take place at FLAME University in Pune from 5–7 August 2026. The meeting will bring together researchers, AI specialists, nutrition experts, and implementation partners to review the current state of the AIMM system and prepare the upcoming large-scale training data collection in Maharashtra.

18.04.2026

Completion of the first AIMM pilot study in Nashik

The first working version of the AIMM smartphone application was successfully tested with more than 150 children during the pilot study in Nashik district, Maharashtra. The pilot focused on image acquisition, anthropometric workflows, and operational feasibility in community settings. The system will now be refined ahead of the large-scale training data collection planned for 2026.

04.03.2026

Interview: AIMM and the Center for Artificial Intelligence

Researchers from AIMM discussed the role of smartphone-based AI systems for community nutrition monitoring and the challenges of deploying computer vision systems in low-resource settings.

27.02.2026

New publication: D2A in Frontiers in Public Health

A new paper presenting the D2A smartphone workflow for community-led malnutrition screening in Kenya was published in Frontiers in Public Health.
