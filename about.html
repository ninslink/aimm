About AIMM

Visual AI for child nutrition monitoring.

AIMM develops smartphone-based visual anthropometry around three design goals: accuracy in nutritional assessment, usability in field conditions, and scalability for repeated monitoring.

The AIMM approach combines image capture, pose-guided body detection, anthropometric feature extraction, and AI-assisted inference into a field-oriented monitoring system.

Accuracy · Usability · Scalability
01

Accuracy

Pose-guided detection, structured body features, and model validation improve the reliability of image-based nutritional assessment.

02

Usability

The system is designed for smartphone capture, clear field protocols, and simple workflows for enumerators and caregivers.

03

Scalability

Repeated household or community screening can support monitoring over time without relying on specialized equipment.
