Artificial Intelligence for Monitoring Malnutrition

AI-powered child nutrition monitoring

AIMM builds a smartphone-based approach to detect and monitor child acute malnutrition using AI and imaging—designed for low-resource settings where conventional screening is hard to scale.

A caregiver- and community-facing workflow that combines computer vision with anthropometric indicators (e.g., MUAC, WHZ proxies) to support faster and more frequent nutrition monitoring.

AI Child nutrition Low-resource settings Smartphone Infrared imaging Open-source

Malnutrition can escalate quickly—timely, granular data enables earlier detection and better targeted support, especially during crises and in remote areas.
