Project

Artificial Intelligence for Monitoring Malnutrition (AIMM) develops and validates a smartphone-based system for detecting acute malnutrition in children using image analysis combined with anthropometric indicators.

The project aims to enable caregivers and community health workers to conduct reliable nutritional assessments using a smartphone, improving early detection and monitoring of malnutrition in low-resource settings.
